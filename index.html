About Me

I am a master's student in the Erasmus Mundus program on Language and Communication Technology (EMLCT). As part of this program, I am integrated into a different local program each year of my master's degree. I passed my first year at the Université de Lorraine in the Natural Language Processing (NLP) master's program. I am now passing my second year at Universität des Saarlandes in the Language and Science Technology (LST) master's. I pursued my undergraduate degree at Indiana Unviersity where I received Bachelor degrees in both Computational Linguistics and Psychology with minors in Computer Science, Economics, and Second Language Studies.
